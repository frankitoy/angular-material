About our Privacy Policy

Our mission is to help people easily find and access trusted recommendations from within their personal networks. For that reason we have created a personal recommendation platform for skills and services, where you can showcase skills & services, and share recommendations within your crowd. This policy describes what information we collect and how it is used and shared.

Information we collect

From and about you We collect the content and information you provide when you become ZippyCrowd member. The information you provide when you create your account may include your photo, your name, email, location, contact number, short bio and skills. We also collect content and information you share and communicate with other members.

From and about others We collect the content and information that other people provide when they use our Services, including information about you, such as when they recommend you into an Ask post, share your posts with their ZippyCrowd friends and their social networks, send a message to you, or zip you to their crowds of trusted and recommended people.

From and about people you are connected here We collect information about the people you are connected to (Your Crowds) and how you interact with them, such as the people you communicate with the most or recommend them by zipping to your crowd and recommending them into posts.

Devices you use to access We collect information from or about the computers, phones, or other devices where you access our Services. Here are some examples of the device information we collect:

  • Attributes such as the operating system, hardware version, device settings, file and software names and types, battery and signal strength, and device identifiers.
  • Device locations, including specific geographic locations, such as through GPS, Bluetooth, or WiFi signals.
  • Connection information such as the name of your mobile operator or ISP, browser type, mobile phone number and IP address.

Third Party Websites There may be some places on our service where you may click on a link to access third party websites that operate under their own Privacy Policies, and may independently solicit and collect information. For example, if you click on an advertisement on our Service, you may be taken to a website that we do not control. We recommend that you view the privacy policies of all third-party websites you visit.

We may allow service providers, advertising companies and ad networks, and other third parties to display advertisements on our Service. These companies may use tracking technologies, such as cookies or web beacons, to collect information about users who view or interact with their advertisements. We do not provide any non-anonymized personal information to third parties.

How we track data

We use various technologies to collect information from your device and about your activities on our Site.

Information collected automatically. We automatically collect information from your browser or device when you visit our Site. This information could include your IP address, device ID and type, your browser type and language, the operating system used by your device, access times, your mobile device’s geographic location while our application is actively running, and the referring website address.

Cookies and Use of Cookie Data. When you visit the Site, we may use industry-wide technologies such as "cookies", which store certain information on your computer and which will allow us to enable automatic sign-in to the Site, make your browsing much more convenient and allow us to offer you personalized experience on the Site. Cookies (which may be html files, Flash files or other technology) are text files that help store user preferences and activity. The Site uses cookies to collect statistical data about its use, to tailor the Site's functionality to suit personal preferences and to assist with various aspects of Site operation. These files contain a variety of information such as information about web pages visited by you, the length of time you visited certain sites, data about how you came to visit particular sites, the scope and nature of those sites, areas viewed by you, and additional information.

Blocking Cookies You may set your browser to block all cookies, including cookies associated with our services, or to indicate when a cookie is being set by us. However, it's important to remember that many of our services may not function properly if your cookies are disabled. For example, cookies may, in certain cases, save you from the need to enter usernames and passwords, and allow session continuity.

How we use the information we collect

All information we collect helps us provide, develop and further improve ZippyCrowd Site. By using this information we are able to help you connect with people who are important to you, find trusted skilled people & recommendations you are interested in, and make suggestions for you how to get the most out of our Site. We are able to add social validation of your skills, services and searches through features such as your Reputation rating. We also collect your feedback, test features, conduct troubleshooting and analyze the information we have to improve and add features and services to improve opportunities for you on ZippyCrowd.

In order for us to provide you with our core services for free, we do need a stream of income from advertising. Our promise to you is to display to you as few and as relevant ads as possible. Our core services are always free. Your information allows us to tailor advertising and to show you the most relevant and sponsored content and promotions.

The information we collect about you to will be used to:

  • manage your account and provide you with customer support;
  • add social validation of your skills, services and Reputation rating through features such as “Zip to my crowd”, “Asking Recommendations (Asking Recs)”, “Open to Offers” and “Recommend”
  • perform research and analysis about your use of, or interest in, our or other's products, services, or content;
  • communicate with you by email, postal mail, telephone and/or mobile devices about products or services that may be of interest to you either from us or other third parties;
  • send you important updates about our Site, policies, new features, hot deals & promotional stuff;
  • develop, display, and track content and advertising tailored to your interests on our Site and other sites, including providing our advertisements to you when you visit other sites;
  • conduct website or mobile application analytics;
  • enforce or exercise any rights in our Terms of Use and
  • perform functions or services as otherwise described to you at the time of collection.
