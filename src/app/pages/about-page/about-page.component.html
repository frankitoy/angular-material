The Power of Trusted Relationships

Why recommendation is the key

Throughout the history people have used word-of-mouth as the easiest way to make decisions. Jobs big or small, tasks, errands, gigs or personal services – they all require someone who is the right fit, and whom you can trust, especially if you need to let them into your personal space.

In today’s society, everyone - you, me, our favorite hairdresser or accountant - are fighting for attention, trying to break through the noise and clutter of the crowded and brutally competitive online space. Getting noticed in the noise is incredibly hard, if not impossible.

Everyone is having it tough.

  • People’s profiles, skills and services are scattered across numerous job sites, service platforms, marketplaces, professional networks, groups, social sites.
  • Professionals, self-employed, freelancers are struggling to promote themselves.
  • The hunt for the right person is a draining, tedious, extremely time-consuming and totally inefficient process of phone calls, messages, social posts, searches, reading reviews - all done in an attempt to mitigate the risk of letting some stranger into your business or personal space.
  • The potential of skills and recommendations within personal networks is massively underutilized.

At ZippyCrowd, we believe there is a better way. Our easy to use, online recommendation tool and platform helps you find people you need, and get recommended by people you trust.

Here are a few of the many things that you can expect:

  • The ability to find, promote and monetize any skill and service
  • Recommendations from within your crowd and personal networks
  • Tools to help you keep all recommendations in one place, at your fingertips, organized - never forgetting the name of a tradie, or losing a business card

ZippyCrowd is a game changer, a leap forward. Far easier to sift through and get to the right person than social sites with their collections of selfies, likes and “eyeballs”. Far more personal than various marketplaces that view people as prospects, bids, and one off faceless transactions.

We founded ZippyCrowd to unlock the power of your trusted relationships .

We designed ZippyCrowd to be friendly, smart and helpful. To cut through the noise, eliminate the risk, give you an edge, and make your life a little less stressful.

We are cracking the code of personal recommendations, and adding more features as we go, to help you focus on the people that matter.

We are a team of passionate thinkers, askers, doers and recommenders. At our core, we believe:

  • that one trusted recommendation can create hundreds of opportunities
  • that “trust” doesn’t show up in the results when searching for a good babysitter, hairdresser, accountant, mechanic, personal trainer, tradie and more
  • that recommending forward is good for the soul and real relationships

We’re just getting started, and we want to invite you to join us on a change-making journey.
